K&S Study Guide
================

An interactive quiz app built from Kaplan & Sadock's Study Guide and
Self-Examination Review — 602 questions across all 34 chapters, each
linked directly to its correct answer and full explanation.

HOW TO START
------------
Double-click "index.html" in this folder. It opens in your default
web browser and works completely offline — no install, no internet
connection, no account needed.

MODES
-----
- Quiz:   answer questions one at a time with instant feedback.
- Test:   answer a full block with no feedback until you finish,
          then see your score and review every question.
- Browse: read questions and reveal the answer/explanation on demand,
          no scoring.

You can filter by chapter, flag questions to revisit later, and any
question you get wrong is automatically saved to the "Incorrect" tab
for spaced review.

YOUR PROGRESS
-------------
Everything (answers, flags, incorrect list, current position) saves
automatically in your browser as you go — there's no save button and
nothing to remember. Progress is stored locally on your own computer
and browser, so:
  - It stays private to you; nothing is uploaded anywhere.
  - Keep this folder in one place — moving or renaming it will make
    your browser treat it as a new page and your progress won't
    carry over.
  - Don't open it in a private/incognito window, since that storage
    disappears when the window closes.

Enjoy studying!
